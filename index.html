ABOUT US

The name Skill-o-Pedia is taken in context to “Wikipedia” which helps us to gain any type of information on any topic. Similarly, Skill-o-Pedia will help the consumer by recommending the skills needed for him/her to advance on the next level in their domain and find Suitable Employment. It will provide equal opportunities for the Skilled candidates as well as help the Ones to enhance their skills. The providers of the skill courses will be introduced to advance yet easy to use tools to reach out to more of the crowd in need.

Benefits for Candidates

  • Recommendation of the Skills

  • Courses/Trainings offered by Skill-o-Pedia

  • Online as well as Offline Teaching

  • Job and Internship Recommendations.

Benefits for Providers

  • Enhanced Video Player

  • Audio/Caption Translator

  • Live Classroom

  • Suggest Targeted audience

  • Suggestions for Topics in Demand
